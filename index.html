Emirates Travel Hackathon

San Francisco, CA

In this hackathon, my teammates and I built a browser extension known as Aerosearch, which allows users to actually reverse lookup on any images they found on the internet, which later suggests the location, and displays a calender with prices to the destination, in a way that allows the user to book their flights without any hassle.

We used APIs from Google Custom Search and Stripe.

The Second Thought Bot

Sunnyvale, CA.

The Second Thought Bot is a sentiment analysis chatbot for businesses in Yelp, Facebook, and etc. Businesses can suffer greatly from negative online reviews about their businesses and services. By reviewing a sentiment of a customer's post in real-time, while it is being written by them, we allow businesses to intercept a negative post before it goes online, thus allowing the business to remedy the customer's concerns. The Second Thought Bot uses HPE Haven OnDemand and a chatbot to provide remedy to this solution. Analyzing a series of sentiment and injecting the chatbot to engage with a customer is just the start. The HPE Haven OnDemand suite of APIs allows us to categorize reviews, identify key trends, and determine in which categories (Food, Service, etc.) the sentiment is trending up vs. trending down.

You can look at the sample at TheSecondThoughtBot.com

3D SHOPPING

University of California, San Diego

3D Shopping is an augmented reality app to allow users to experience a 3-dimensional view of an object while users are doing their online shopping on a website. Basically, any users can use this app in their phone, place their phone in front of a online shopping website, then click on the image and a 3-dimensional view of the item will pop up on their phone.

This project was built in 36 hours at SD Hacks, a collegiate hackathon organized by UC San Diego. Although my teammates and I had little hope of winning in this hackathon, we turned out to be one of the five finalists to pitch our idea in front of the panel judges.

You can preview the work at devpost.com

SWOOP

University of California, Davis

SWOOP is an on demand ride sharing app. Our mission is to make getting to destinations easier, affordable, and more eco-friendly than ever. Unlike other ride share pages and apps, Swoop offers a real time on-demand ride sharing service. Simply open the app at any time and view all the commuters headed to your desired destination. You will then be able to request a ride for a small price that we guarantee will be a fraction of what you pay for an Uber or Lyft. The reason our prices are so low is because they are set by commuters like you and me, who are just looking for a little gas money and are generous enough to pick you up on their way! I worked mostly on creating the payment system using Stripe API and implemented Google Maps API to find the nearest route to pick up any commuters heading to the same destination as the driver.

You can preview the work at devpost.com
